# Weekly Review

Help me review the week and plan the next one.

## Instructions for Claude

1. Read `@me/goals.md` for active goals
2. Read `@me/focus.md` for current priorities
3. Scan this week's journal entries
4. Check `@inbox/` for unprocessed items

## Conversation Flow

### 1. Week in Review

**Wins**
- What went well this week?
- What am I proud of?

**Challenges**
- What didn't go as planned?
- What got in the way?

**Learnings**
- What did I learn?
- Any patterns I notice?

### 2. Goal Progress

Review each active goal area:
- Work (LEA)
- Ventures
- Personal
- Learning

For each: What progress was made? What's blocking?

### 3. Inbox Processing

Go through `inbox/` items:
- Delete: No longer relevant
- Do: Takes < 2 minutes
- Delegate: Someone else should handle
- Defer: Schedule for later
- File: Move to appropriate location

### 4. Next Week Planning

- What are the 3 most important things for next week?
- Any deadlines or commitments?
- What would make next week great?

### 5. Energy & Sustainability

- Average energy this week?
- What patterns helped or hurt?
- Any adjustments needed?

## Output

1. Create weekly review entry in `reviews/weekly/YYYY-WXX.md`
2. Update `me/focus.md` with next week's priorities
3. Process inbox items
4. Flag any goals that need attention
