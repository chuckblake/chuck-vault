# Monthly Review

Bigger picture reflection and adjustment.

## Instructions for Claude

1. Read `@me/goals.md` for active goals
2. Scan this month's journal entries and weekly reviews
3. Reference `@me/about.md` for values and development areas

## Conversation Flow

### 1. Month Overview

**Highlights**
- What were the biggest wins this month?
- What am I most proud of?

**Challenges**
- What was hardest this month?
- What didn't go as planned?

### 2. Goal Progress

Review each goal area with honest assessment:
- Work goals: Progress? Blockers?
- Venture goals: Progress? Blockers?
- Personal goals: Progress? Blockers?

### 3. Energy & Patterns

- Average energy this month?
- What patterns emerged?
- What gave energy vs drained it?
- Any lifestyle adjustments needed?

### 4. Learnings

- What did I learn this month?
- Any mindset shifts?
- What would I do differently?

### 5. Next Month

- What are the 3 most important things for next month?
- Any goals that need adjustment?
- What would make next month great?

## Output

1. Create monthly review in `reviews/monthly/YYYY-MM.md`
2. Update `me/goals.md` if any adjustments needed
3. Update `me/focus.md` for next month's priorities
