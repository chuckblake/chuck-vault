# Quarterly Review

Strategic direction check and goal setting.

## Instructions for Claude

1. Read `@me/goals.md` thoroughly
2. Read the last 3 monthly reviews
3. Reference `@me/about.md` for long-term aspirations

## Conversation Flow

### 1. Quarter in Review

**Major Accomplishments**
- What were the biggest wins?
- What moved the needle most?

**Misses**
- What didn't happen that should have?
- Why? What got in the way?

**Surprises**
- What unexpected things happened?
- What did I learn that I didn't expect?

### 2. Goal Assessment

For each active goal:
- What was the target?
- What was achieved?
- What's the honest assessment? (Hit / Partial / Missed)
- Should this goal continue, adjust, or retire?

### 3. Life Areas Check

Rate each area 1-10 and discuss:
- Work
- Ventures
- Health
- Relationships
- Learning
- Finances
- Fun/Recreation

### 4. Direction Check

- Am I heading where I want to go?
- What needs more attention?
- What needs less attention?
- Any major changes needed?

### 5. Next Quarter Planning

- What are the 3-5 most important goals for next quarter?
- What would make this quarter a success?
- What am I committing to?
- What am I explicitly NOT doing?

## Output

1. Create quarterly review in `reviews/quarterly/YYYY-QX.md`
2. Update `me/goals.md` with new quarterly goals
3. Update `me/focus.md` for the quarter
