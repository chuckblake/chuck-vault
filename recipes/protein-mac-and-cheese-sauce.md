# Protein Mac & Cheese Sauce

High-protein cheese sauce using cottage cheese as a base. Great over pasta, vegetables, or as a dip.

## Ingredients

- 16 oz (454g) cottage cheese
- 2/3 cup (67g) shredded cheddar
- 1/4 cup (25g) shredded Parmesan
- 1/4 cup (28g) cheddar powder
- 5/8 cup (150g) milk
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Add cottage cheese to blender and blend until smooth
2. Add shredded cheddar, Parmesan, and cheddar powder
3. Add milk gradually while blending
4. Blend until completely smooth
5. Heat in saucepan over medium-low, stirring constantly
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Serve over cooked pasta or use as desired

## Notes

- For extra smooth sauce, strain cottage cheese before blending
- Adjust milk for desired thickness
- Add a pinch of mustard powder or garlic for more flavor
