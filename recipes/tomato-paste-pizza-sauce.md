# Tomato Paste Pizza Sauce

Simple no-cook pizza sauce made from tomato paste.

## Ingredients

- 1 small can tomato paste
- 10g sugar
- 2g salt
- 1g pepper
- 1g onion powder
- 1g garlic powder
- Pinch of basil
- Pinch of oregano
- 10g oil
- 150g water

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
2. Mix until smooth and uniform
3. Adjust seasoning to taste
4. Use immediately or refrigerate

## Notes

- Great for quick homemade pizza nights
- Can be made ahead and stored in fridge
