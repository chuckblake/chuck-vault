# Black Bean Protein Bars

High-protein snack bars made with black beans. Dense, fudgy, and packed with protein.

## Ingredients

- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup oats
- 4 dates, pitted
- 4 scoops protein powder
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 1/4 cup sweetener (monk fruit, stevia, etc.)
- Egg whites (to bind)
- Chocolate chips for topping (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300째F (150째C)
2. Add all ingredients except chocolate chips to food processor
3. Blend until smooth and combined
4. Line a bread pan with parchment paper
5. Pour mixture into pan and spread evenly
6. Top with chocolate chips if desired
7. Bake for 40 minutes until set (check center with toothpick)
8. Let cool completely before slicing into bars
