## Section 5: The Format

Simple but powerful. Add as much or as little detail as you want:

```markdown
- [ ] Simple task

- [ ] Task with context @Project #tag !priority

- [ ] Full details @Work #urgent !high +Boss ~30min &Career
  Notes: Include Q4 projections
  They need this before the board meeting
```

**Six optional symbols** â€” define what they mean for your workflow:

| Symbol | You decide |
|--------|------------|
| `@` | Project, client, area |
| `#` | Tag, label, category |
| `!` | Priority, energy, mood |
| `+` | Person, context, place |
| `~` | Time, estimate, size |
| `&` | Goal, theme, quarter |

Configure what shows on the list vs. detail view.
