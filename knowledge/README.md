# Knowledge

Learning, reading, and reference material. This is where synthesis happens.

## Structure

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `articles/` | Saved articles, highlights, commentary |
| `books/` | Book notes and summaries |
| `notes/` | Topic-based notes, concepts, syntheses |

## What Goes Here

- Article highlights and takeaways
- Book notes and summaries
- Concept explanations
- Synthesized notes connecting multiple sources
- Reference material you want to remember

## vs Inbox

**Inbox:** Raw capture, unprocessed
**Knowledge:** Processed, organized, synthesized

## Article Template

```markdown
# Article Title

**Source:** [URL]
**Author:**
**Date Read:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Tags:** #topic1 #topic2

## Key Takeaways
- Point 1
- Point 2

## Highlights
> Quote or highlight

## My Thoughts
What this means for me, how it connects to other ideas.

## Action Items
- [ ] Anything to do based on this
```

## Tips

- Don't just save - capture WHY it matters
- Connect to other notes and ideas
- Review periodically to refresh memory
- Quality > quantity
