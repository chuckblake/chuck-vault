# Silicon Alley 30th Anniversary "OG Founder Hosts" Research

**Event Date:** January 30, 2026
**Saved:** January 23, 2026

## Overview
These individuals were listed as "OG Founder Hosts" for the Silicon Alley 30th anniversary celebration (January 30, 2026). They represent pioneering figures in New York City's tech ecosystem from the mid-1990s onward.

## Researched Profiles

### Adam Seifer
**Current Role:** Co-Founder and Co-CEO of Everplans
**Background:** Co-founded Fotolog in 2002, served as Chief Product Officer. Founded Everplans, a digital life planning platform. Early Silicon Alley entrepreneur focused on social media and user interaction.
**Note:** There are multiple Adam Seifers; the relevant one is the tech entrepreneur, not the Charlotte-based attorney.

### Alain Bankier
**Current Role:** Angel Investor, Active member of New York Angels
**Background:** Co-founder of New York Angels (precursor founded in mid-1990s). Former CEO of Manischewitz Company. Operating Partner at Laconia Capital Group. Serial entrepreneur with 20+ years in early-stage investing. Focus on food tech, cannabis, and technology investments. BA in Psychology from University of Pennsylvania, BS in Economics from Wharton, MBA from NYU and HEC Paris.
**Notable Investments:** Plated (acquired by Albertsons), various tech and food companies

### Alan Patricof
**Current Role:** Co-Founder and Chairman of Primetime Partners; Chairman Emeritus of Greycroft
**Background:** Legendary venture capitalist, approximately 90 years old and still active. Founded Patricof & Co. Ventures in 1969 (predecessor to Apax Partners). Founded Greycroft in 2006 ($2B+ AUM). Member of Biden's SIPC board. Early investor in Apple, AOL, Office Depot, Audible. Served in Bush and Obama administrations on Millennium Challenge Corporation board.
**Recognition:** Called "the man who paved the way for Silicon Valley" by Crain's

### Alexis Maybank
**Current Role:** Founder & CEO of Creative Beauty; Board Member of 2U Inc., Girls Who Code
**Background:** Co-founded Gilt Groupe in 2007 (with Alexandra Wilkis Wilson, Kevin Ryan, Mike Bryzek, Phong Nguyen). Served as founding CEO. Early eBay employee - launched eBay Canada and helped found eBay Motors. Prior GM at AOL e-commerce. Co-founded Project September. Harvard BS (Environmental Science), Harvard MBA.
**Publications:** Co-author "By Invitation Only: How We Built Gilt and Changed the Way Millions Shop"

### Andrew McLaughlin
**Current Role:** COO at SandboxAQ; Co-Founder and Partner at Higher Ground Labs
**Background:** Former Deputy Chief Technology Officer under President Obama (2009-2011). First Head of Global Public Policy at Google (2003-2009). Helped launch ICANN (1999-2002) as VP/CFO. CEO of Digg and Instapaper (betaworks companies). Board member of Globant. Venture partner at betaworks. Former President & COO of Assembly OSM (2019-2023).
**Education:** BA from Yale, JD from Harvard Law

### Andrew Weinreich
**Current Role:** Serial entrepreneur; Chairman of Indicative; runs Andrew's Roadmaps
**Background:** Founded SixDegrees.com in 1997 - the first social networking website, sold for $125 million in 1999. Holds first patent on social networking. Founded 7 startups total including MeetMoi (location-based dating, sold to Match.com parent) and Xtify (sold to IBM). Hosts "Predicting Our Future" podcast.

### Anil Dash
**Current Role:** VP of Developer Experience at Fastly; Head of Glitch
**Background:** Blogging pioneer (since 1999). Former CEO of Glitch (Fog Creek Software), first tech startup to voluntarily recognize workers' union. Co-created early NFTs with Kevin McCoy. First employee at Six Apart. Advisor to Obama White House Office of Digital Strategy. Board member: EFF, The Markup, Lower East Side Girls Club. Contributing editor for Wired. Prince scholar, Time named him one of best Twitter accounts.
**Recognition:** 2022 Webby Lifetime Achievement Award


### Bo Peabody
**Current Role:** Co-Founder and Executive Chairman of Seated; Venture Partner at Greycroft; Board Partner
**Background:** Co-founded Tripod.com in 1992 (one of first social networks), sold to Lycos for $58M in 1998. Co-founded Village Ventures (1999), Everyday Health (founding chairman, sold to Ziff Davis), VoodooVox, HealthGuru Media, UplayMe. Co-owns Mezze Restaurant Group. Author of "Lucky or Smart?"
**Education:** Williams College (Political Science), 1994

### Chris Fralic
**Current Role:** Board Partner at First Round Capital (since 2019; Partner 2006-2019)
**Background:** 35+ years tech experience. Early employee at Half.com (1999), VP of Business Development. VP of Business Development at del.icio.us through Yahoo acquisition. 6 years at eBay post-acquisition. Investments include Roblox, Warby Parker, Ring (acquired by Amazon), HotelTonight (acquired by Airbnb), DoubleVerify, Flurry (Yahoo), Invite Media (Google), Demdex (Adobe). Helped launch TEDTalks, attended TED Conference 25+ years.
**Education:** BS Finance from Villanova, MBA from Saint Joseph's University

### Chris Hughes
**Current Role:** Pursuing PhD in Economics at Wharton School; Chair of Economic Security Project
**Background:** Co-founded Facebook with Mark Zuckerberg at Harvard (2004), left 2007, made $500M at IPO. Directed online organizing for Obama 2008 campaign (My.BarackObama.com). Owned The New Republic (2012-2016). Co-founded Economic Security Project (2016). Advocate for breaking up Facebook (2019). Published "Fair Shot" (2018) and "Marketcrafters: The 100-Year Struggle to Shape the American Economy" (2025).
**Education:** Harvard BA (History and Literature, magna cum laude)

### Craig Newmark
**Current Role:** Full-time Philanthropist; Founder of Craig Newmark Philanthropies
**Background:** Founded Craigslist in 1995, CEO 1999-2000, retired 2018. Now focuses on philanthropy: cybersecurity ($100M pledge), military families and veterans ($25M+ to Bob Woodruff Foundation), trustworthy journalism, pigeon rescue. Signed Giving Pledge (December 2025). Has given away $450M+, donated all Craigslist equity ($392M) to 501c4. No longer a billionaire due to giving. Born 1952.
**Education:** Case Western Reserve BS and MS in Computer Science (1975, 1977)


### Dennis Crowley
**Current Role:** Co-Founder of Hopscotch Labs (2024); Founder/Chairman of Kingston Stockade FC
**Background:** Co-founded Dodgeball (2003, sold to Google 2005). Co-founded Foursquare (2009), was CEO until 2016, Executive Chairman until 2021. Co-founded LivingCities (2022, with Matt Miesnieks and John Gaeta). In 2024, co-founded Hopscotch Labs (with Max Sklar and Alejandro Fragoso), creating BeeBot app. Board co-chair at Foursquare. Named Fortune "40 Under 40" (2010 & 2011).
**Education:** Syracuse BA (1998), NYU Tisch MPS (2004)

### Scott Heiferman
**Current Role:** Former Chairman of Meetup (stepped down ~2019)
**Background:** Co-founded Meetup in 2002 (inspired by 9/11), CEO until 2018. Founded i-traffic (1995, online ad agency, sold to Agency.com). Co-founded Fotolog. Meetup acquired by WeWork in 2017 for ~$200M. Moved to Chairman role 2018, departed when WeWork sold Meetup (~2019). Meetup grew to 40M+ members, 320K groups.
**Education:** University of Iowa, BA Marketing (1994)
